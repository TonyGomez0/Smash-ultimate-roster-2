The fandom of Smash bros. ultimate

The overall concept of Smash Bros. Ultimate is how the characters from different franchises fight against each other within different rule sets, stages, and/or items from their represented franchises.

The creator of this masterpiece is Masaihro Sakurai and has been making this series into a success since 1999.

The mechanics of Smash Bros:

  • Directional air dodging
  • Hitstun
  • airials
  • Specials(3 in total)
  • Sheilding (Many options)
  • Grabbing
  • DI (Directional influence)
  • Edge options
  • Rage
